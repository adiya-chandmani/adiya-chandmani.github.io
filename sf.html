The Effenciety of Wind Turbine Blade Design

Our Science Fair project is called “The Efficiency of Wind Turbine Blade Designs." In this project, we work on the efficiency of different blade designs. As an example, me and Jay made blades with different shapes. If I generated 0.75 volts, and Jay generated 0.54 volts. This shows that my blades are more efficient than Jay's. Which is my blade is produce more energy than Jay’s blade.

Engineering Design Process

Ask

"What can we do to save the planet?" The project, started by the our question, uses wind energy to help save the planet by reducing the use of fossil fuels.

Imagine

We thought about how to make the most energy from a limited amount of wind. We realized that different blade designs generate different amounts of energy

Plan

We each planned to create our blade designs on a 3D design website called "Tinkerkad".

Create

We used a 3D printer to print our respective 3D models, namely the blade design. However, our first model failed.

Exmperiment

We fixed the issues with the blades and experimented. In our experiment, Jay generated 0.67 volts, Terry generated 0.37 volts, and Siwen generated 0.36 volts. This shows that Jay's blade is the most efficient.

Improve

We will be creating blade designs with more energy generation than Jay's in the near future. We'll also fix the problems with our design so that we can come up with a better blade design next time.
